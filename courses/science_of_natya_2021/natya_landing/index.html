About the university

Karnataka Samskrit University was established in 2010 by the Karnataka Government for the development of Samskrit language. The university strives to consolidate, revive and promote the Bhāratīya Jñāna Paramparā (Indian Knowledge Systems) and also to integrate the scientific thoughts contained in the Vedas and Samskrit Literature. The university aims to function as a high-level teaching and research institution in various śāstras such as Vyākaraṇa, Sāṅkhya, Yōga, Nyāya, Vaiśēṣika, Mīmāṁsā, Vēdānta, Bauddha, Jaina, Dharma śāstra and others.
